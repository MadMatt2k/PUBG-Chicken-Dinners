Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

PUBG Chicken Dinners is a Discord bot designed to provide PUBG match tracking, statistics, ranking images, and gameplay-related features for Discord communities.

Information We Collect

The bot may store the following information:

  • Discord server IDs
  • Discord channel IDs
  • Discord user IDs
  • PUBG account IDs and usernames
  • PUBG match statistics and telemetry data
  • Server configuration settings

How Information Is Used

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Providing bot functionality
  • Generating match reports and ranking images
  • Tracking PUBG statistics
  • Storing server preferences and bot settings
  • Improving bot performance and reliability

Data Sharing

No personal information is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Data may be processed through official third-party services required for the bot to function, including Discord, PUBG APIs, and PUBG.report.

Data Storage

Data is stored only as long as necessary to provide bot functionality. Server administrators may request removal of stored server-related data.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information and prevent unauthorized access. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Contact

For support or privacy-related questions, contact the bot developer through Discord.
